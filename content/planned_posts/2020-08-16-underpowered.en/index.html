Free throws: Lebron James 73.4% https://www.basketball-reference.com/players/j/jamesle01.html Shaquille O’Neal: 52.7% https://www.basketball-reference.com/players/o/onealsh01.html (https://www.stat.ubc.ca/~rollin/stats/ssize/b2.html would need to observe 85 before we were confident).

Barry Bonds had an almost impossible 6.5 AB/HR. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/At_bats_per_home_run

A typical team would average about 24 AB/HR. https://www.teamrankings.com/mlb/stat/at-bats-per-home-run (We would need to observe 109 at bats. It seems most players can expect about 4.5 at bats per game, so we might need 24 games of watching these mystery players to be actually reasonably confident we are watching the most absurd statistical anomaly season ever, rather than just a typical modern hitter.
